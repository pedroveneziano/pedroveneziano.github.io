Resident Zine

Illustration zine made as promotion material for a magazine series called Resident Magazine, all developed during a publication class I took at Parsons.

  • — Next Project

The idea was to get one of the cover illustrations — the strongest source of identity on the publication — and unfold it into a new material. I used its elements and the seamless red environment to create a series of humoristic illustrations.
At the moment I was really keen to create a zine (since some friends were developing some really great stuff), but found it hard to relate it with my work language. So I saw an opportunity in the minimal 3D illustrations and came up this 20-page small red zine.
