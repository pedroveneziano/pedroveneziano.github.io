R Misto

R Design is a Design congress made by students every year in different cities throughout Brazil. In 2014, Unesp's students decided to host the event in Bauru.

  • — Next Project

Traditionally, the starting point for this event's creation is picking an unique theme that highlights its dynamics. For this edition, the theme 'Misto' (mixed), a word that's already associated to Bauru due to a joky well-known expression, was chosen to transmit what was being proposed for this event: to blend different areas and knowlegdes that could be strongly associated to Design and, mainly, to recognize the importance of living and experiencing in the formation of our cultural knowledge. The main symbol, quietly combining the letters R and M, talks about connexions, layers, overlays, bonds.
