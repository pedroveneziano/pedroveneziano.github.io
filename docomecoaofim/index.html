Do Começo ao Fim

Key visual created for a workshop on Publication Design and creative process that I gave during N Design São Paulo in 2015.

  • — Next Project

Each book cover is conceptually related to a different topic to be discussed (from a brief personal introduction to the practical part of the workshop), so they were slowly revealed as I went through the whole presentation. The initial setup was used as several openers for the topics (each introducing a new book), while the other images result from composition studies as I explored the interaction between objects and environment.
