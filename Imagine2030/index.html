Imagine 2030

Imagine 2030 is a project dedicated to research and promote ways to achieve the 17 goals of sustainable development, set globally by the United Nations. The project invited three artists to create illustrations inspired by this future, bringing awareness to the cause through an exhibition in São Paulo’s main avenue.
This illustration aims to visualize the idea of co-existence, a key aspect when it comes to significant changes. Multiple communities, multiple actions, economy and sustainability working together. A combination of factors that can only thrive when grown in unison.
