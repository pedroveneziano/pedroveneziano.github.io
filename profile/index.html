Pedro is a Brazilian graphic designer and art director focused on exploring diverse visual approaches to create expressive graphic projects and eye-catching imagery. With a range of skills and a particular interest in the 2D/3D dialogue, he plays with form, texture, typography and color to reach and elevate the creative potential of each work.
   He studied graphic design at Universidade Estadual Paulista and graduated in 2015, going through a one-year program at The New School in the process. So far he has lived and worked in São Paulo, New York and Barcelona, crafting detailed still and motion projects for digital, editorial and visual communication purposes.
