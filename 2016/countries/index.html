Countries

Series of 3D illustrations made to be used as covers for a publication. Each composition refers to a different country by portraying objects related to its culture, people and habits.

  • — Next Project

To make them as a series I followed one basic rule: each illustration would be made out of three different materials — a white matte, a shiny/metalic material and a more textured one. I also picked colors and surfaces with the intention of evoking each country's aesthetics in a way.
