Tempo Tempo

Experimental project made for a Creative Computing class at Parsons. My proposal was to create a non-linear interactive narrative inspired by Alan Lightman's book, 'Einstein's Dreams'.

  • — Next Project

Each page is a visual interpretation of a specific concept of time proposed by the author. My main goal at the time was to come up with a Processing project that would exist in its own environment as an interactive online experience, rather than being hosted on a developer's website or working as a site-specific experience. I also wanted to explore different aesthetics that were all conected, but at the same time each visual should relate to a different concept. To access/exit the different stories, simply press the indicated keys. Also press X for instructions and concept descriptions.
