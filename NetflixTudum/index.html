Netflix Tudum

Visuals created for two different editions of Tudum, a yearly event hosted by Netflix to celebrate its ever-growing community of fans.
The idea behind the first visual is to place the fans as the real stars of the show, picturing them through the lens of everyday personal objects. The keychain highlights easter-eggs from a series of Netflix shows, while adding a layer of nostalgia through subtle hints and overall lighting approach.

  • Client: Netflix
  • Year: 2021-2023
  • Creative and Art Direction: Porto Rocha
  • 3D and Motion: Pedro Veneziano
