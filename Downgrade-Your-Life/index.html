Downgrade Your Life

Year: 2017

Illustration for the Sunday Review section of The New York Times, following an article on deliberately downgrading technology to live a saner life.
My take on the subject was to use our most contemporary symbol of technology submission and diverge it into a dreamlike representation of escapism, visualizing how surreal the idea of downgrading technology initially seems.
