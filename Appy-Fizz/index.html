Appy Fizz

Year: 2016

Appy Fizz is a sparkling apple juice drink available in India. The product's new identity visualizes the fizzing sensation of the drink through a dynamic graphic language of dots, taking over shapes and scenarios.
Along with other graphic artists, I was comissioned to develop a set of imagery that expands the identity into a spatial, vibrant expression. The main campaign images were brought to life with brand ambassador and Bollywood star Priyanka Chopra, while the motion videos highlight the product as a protagonist.
