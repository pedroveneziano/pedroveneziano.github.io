Studio Pharus

Year: 2017

Landing page video created for Studio Pharus, an open studio maintained by Pharus for graphic experimentations, workshops and various activities.
A flexible space that can be used and transformed for various purposes was the main concept to be explored in the creation process. This idea was approached by creating architectural shapes that morph and adapt as the video progresses. The lighting and set are also thought to convey the idea of an immaginative environment, full of possibilites waiting to be thought of.
