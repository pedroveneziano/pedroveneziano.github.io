Brazilian Graphic Designer and Art Director who so far has lived and worked in Bauru (2011—2015), New York (2014), São Paulo (2016) and Barcelona (2017). Using mixed visual languages I create eye-catching imagery for brands, people and projects. Up to now I had the pleasure to work for Youtube, Nike, Adidas, Spotify, Twitter, Coke and others. You can see my previous projects here or check what I’m up to on Instagram.
