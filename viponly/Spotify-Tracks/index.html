Spotify Tracks

Year: 2016

Spotify Tracks is a Facebook video series that showcases a range of Brazilian music artists in every episode, with a different 3-second opening for each video.
My idea was to create something related to the Spotify logo, translating its lines into a dimensional visual that plays with the idea of following music.
