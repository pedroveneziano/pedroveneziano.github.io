Solugen

Solugen is the world's first carbon-negative molecule factory. With a promise to bring clean, sustainable chemistry to every industry and every aspect of modern life, they're converting plant-derived substances into life's essential materials, all with minimal emissions and waste.
In partnership with Landscape, a series of visuals was developed to seamlessly integrate into their new brand narrative and digital experience. The goal was to translate and present Solugen's technologically intricate processes through a striking series of visuals, adding visual interest while keeping the focus on the website content.

  • Client: Solugen
  • Year: 2023
  • Creative Direction: Landscape
  • 3D and Motion: Pedro Veneziano
