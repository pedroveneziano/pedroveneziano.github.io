Legence

Legence is a company dedicated to work along with other businesses to improve the energy efficiency of their buildings. Using the company’s mission as a starting point, a series of motion assets was developed to compliment their newly launched brand identity.

The visuals explore the idea of energy scans, decoding natural elements with a digital aesthetic. In contrast with the robust logo and typography, the visuals hint at the company’s core value: sustainability, seen trough the lens of technology.

  • Client: Legence
  • Year: 2023
  • Developed along with Collins
  • Creative Direction: Farbod Kokabi
  • Art Direction: Morgan Light, Joseph Han
