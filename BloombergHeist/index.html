Bloomberg Businessweek, Heist Issue

Visuals for Bloomberg Businessweek's Heist Issue. Inspired by actual evidence shots, each illustration depict memorabilia from three curious theft stories, from the strange life (and murder) of a rare Stradivarius violin collector to a $85 million ponzi scheme involving rare Air Jordan drops.
